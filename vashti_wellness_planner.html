Weekly meal rhythm
Breakfast (daily)~£0.60
Eggs + toast or fried plantain
Scrambled or boiled eggs with toast on weekdays. Fried eggs with plantain on slower mornings — a proper treat for almost nothing.
Lunch (batch)~£1.20
Tomato & pepper rice soup or tuna pasta
Tomato, pepper, onion, spinach simmered into a light rice soup. Or pasta with tinned tuna, tinned tomatoes, garlic and onion. Both batch well and last 3–4 days.
Dinner Mon / Wed / Fri~£2.00
Jollof rice with chicken thighs
Frozen chicken thighs from Lidl, long-grain rice, tinned tomatoes, scotch bonnet, peppers, onions. Batch for two nights. Your classic.
Dinner Tue / Thu~£1.80
Beef & pepper pasta or egg fried rice
Beef or lamb mince with onions, peppers, tinned tomatoes over pasta — a West African-ish bolognese. Or stir-fried rice with egg, carrots, spinach and soy sauce.
Dinner Saturday~£3–15
Treat night
Grilled fish with rice and sautéed spinach, or whatever you actually fancy. You've earned it.
Weekly shop£30–35
Lidl on Cowley Road
Always stock: eggs, chicken thighs, beef/lamb mince, tinned tuna, tinned tomatoes, rice, pasta, plantain, spinach, peppers, carrots, onions, garlic, tomatoes.

Mind
🧠
University Counselling Service
Free and genuinely good. Register early — there's always a wait. Don't wait until you're struggling; go when things feel manageable too.
🌿
Green space daily
Christ Church Meadow, Port Meadow, University Parks. 30 minutes of natural light and walking does more than most paid wellness things. Free forever.
✨
Protect your mornings
20 minutes before opening your laptop — tea, a slow breakfast, no scrolling. This one habit anchors the whole day.
✦ ✦ ✦
Body
🚲
Cycle everywhere
Secondhand bike from Facebook Marketplace (£50–80). Pays for itself in one month vs buses. Daily movement and mental health boost built in for free.
🏃‍♀️
Running routes
Port Meadow and the Thames Path are stunning. Even 20–30 minutes twice a week transforms mood and sleep. Free, beautiful, yours.
💪
University gym (~£10–15/month)
Heavily subsidised for students. Worth it if structure motivates you. Otherwise, Hybrid Calisthenics on YouTube requires zero equipment and is excellent.
✦ ✦ ✦
Social & joy
🌸
One conscious treat per week
A nice coffee, something from Blackwell's bargain shelf, a long bath with candles. Budget for it so it feels like a reward, not guilt.
👯‍♀️
College & SU events
Many are free or subsidised — film screenings, talks, socials, comedy nights. Social connection is not optional during a DPhil. It is essential.
