On a hard day
When everything feels too much. Come here first.
1
Stop and breathe
4 counts in, hold 4, out 6. Do it three times right now. Your nervous system will shift.
2
Name what you're feeling
Is it research stress? Loneliness? Homesickness? Fatigue? Name it. It's easier to face something you can name.
3
Do the smallest possible thing
Not the whole thesis. Not the whole to-do list. One email. One paragraph. One walk around the block.
4
Call or text someone from home
Homesickness + isolation is real and it compounds. You don't have to explain everything. Just hearing a familiar voice helps.
5
Pray or journal it out
Whatever you're carrying — write it down or bring it to God. You don't have to hold it alone.
6
If it persists — get support
University Counselling Service: 01865 270300. Samaritans: 116 123 (free, 24/7). You deserve real support.
